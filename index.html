About Dianna

I'm not on the other side of the mountain — I'm climbing it with you

So much coaching comes from people who pretend they have it all figured out. I don't, and I won't pretend to. I'm building my business in real time — and building myself right alongside it.

Before I decided to launch this brand from scratch, I spent years in the trenches. I'm an ex-attorney turned business consultant who has managed over $20 million in online advertising spend. I know exactly what works on social media because I've run the data at the highest levels imaginable.

That's exactly why I can help you. I know what it feels like to be mid-transition: when the old version of you doesn't fit anymore and the new one isn't quite built yet. I'll show you the systems, the mindset shifts, and the daily habits that move you forward without burning out.
